How TwoTable works for your venue

On the customer side (dating app):

  • Users match with compatible people inside the TwoTable app.
  • When they’re ready to meet, TwoTable suggests venues that match their preferences (location, budget, vibe, cuisine).
  • We then book a table at a partner venue – you – for their date.

On your side (partner venue):

  • Curated date‑night bookings – couples and small groups who are expecting to spend on a good experience.
  • Off‑peak focus – we steer bookings into quieter slots, so we aren’t competing with your peak‑time covers.
  • No‑show & cancellation protection – deposits and reminders mean fewer no‑shows, and you’re compensated when they do happen.
  • Time‑slot control – you decide which days and hours you want to open to TwoTable.
  • Brand‑safe – no requirement to run heavy public discounts or “voucher” deals.
